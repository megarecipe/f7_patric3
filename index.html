Mexican Mushroom Meat

Description

Ingredients 2-4 servings 1/4 Sweet Onion (chopped) 1/2 Red Bell Pepper (chopped) 5 oz Baby Shitake Mushrooms (whole or roughly chopped) 1 Clove Garlic (finely diced) 6 Sprigs Cilantro finely chopped Juice of 1 Lime 2 Tsp Apple Cider Vinegar 2 Tbsp Paprika 1 Tbsp Dried Parsley 2 Tsp Cumin 1 Tsp Dried Oregano 1 Tsp Dried Dill to taste Salt and Pepper

Cooking

1

Wash all Vegetables, and chop accordingly. Place all ingredients into a Ziploc bag, or food container, and shake to mix well.

2

Marinate in Fridge for 30-90 min.

3

There you go! Ready to use in tacos, sandwiches, dinner platters...YOU name it!
